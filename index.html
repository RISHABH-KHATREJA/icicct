About Institute

Jagan Institute of Management Studies (JIMS) imparts professional education in the fields of Management and Information Technology. The Institute has been working for the attainment of mission: to develop highly skilled and professional human resource for industries and businesses. Our BCA and MCA are affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. JIMS focuses on imparting students with advanced concepts of InfoTech and specialization in the fields like Web-Based solutions, Network Management and other industry related subjects. JIMS is a place for knowledge-driven learners. About the Conference

With Recent developments in the field of Computer Science, Jagan Institute of Management Studies is organizing International Conference on Information, Communication and Computing Technology. The conference will provide forum for research scholars, scientists and engineers to share their experiences in this field.

Scope Of Conference

The conference provides an International forum for students and professionals from academia, industries and other research institutions to discuss their problems and share their achievements in order to improve future concerns in Computer Science.
