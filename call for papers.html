Author Instructions

  • All contributions must be original, should not have been published elsewhere and should not be intended to be published elsewhere during the review period

  • Plagiarism of any type, if detected, will lead to rejection of the articles anytime.

  • At least one author of an accepted paper must register.

    • ICICCT-2018 WILL FOLLOW THE THREE STEP PROCESS:

    STEP I: PAPER FORMAT GUIDELINES

  • The manuscript should be prepared in Springer CCIS, written in English format using MS WORD template.

  • Page size of an article should not exceed 12 in any case. Desired page size is 08 – 10.

  • All references, figures, and tables should be numbered in sequence starting from 1 and MUST be duly cited / referred within the text

  • The figures / graphs / plots in the manuscript MUST be of good resolution (600 dpi or more), tables MUST NOT be in pictorial format. Text in figures should not be too small, and preferably of equal size as text of the article.

  • Any article, not in format, will be subjected to rejection without any judgment on its quality.

    • STEP II: SUBMIT FOR REVIEW

  • Create / sign up for a Springer OCS account if you do not have one.

  • Fill proper metadata of the article in OCS as they exist in your article. Title, Abstract, Keywords, list of authors (in order) and their affiliation should be mentioned in OCS while submitting.

  • You will be assigned a unique article ID of your submitted article, which you should remember and use for any correspondence.

    • STEP III: AFTER ACCEPTANCE

  • Please Note that after Acceptance Notification you cannot widthdraw you paper from ICICCT 2018.

  • Paying Registration Fee: Calculate the registration fee according to your type of authorship (Academia includes Professor / Faculty / Research Scholars / PG Student; Indian Author (Industry); Author from Outside India) and make payment using Demand Draft/Cheque/Online Payments.

  • Do not forget to send scanned copy of the pay-in-slip or Challan or online payment receipt on email: icicct@jimsindia.org alongwith registration form.

    • Mailing the following items in .zip / .rar format to icicct@jimsindia.org :

  • The Camera ready Article files, only in prescribed MS Word format.

  • Scanned copy of filled in Registration Form.

  • Scanned copy of filled in Copyright Form.

  • Proof of studentship.

  • Proof of CSI Membership.

  • Scanned copy of registration fee receipt: Demand Draft/Cheque/Online Payments. The mail must bear the subject line as: “Registration of article ICICCT-2018-XXX”.

    • NOTE:

  • NOTE: DO NOT submit multiple copies of same article, which will cause rejection.

  • Note: Papers are to be submitted through the Springer OCS portal link .

  • NO Review reports will be shared to authors.

Post Conference Proceeding Details

Authors are invited to electronically submit their unpublished research contributions keeping in mind the relevance of conference tracks. The submitted papers through Springer OCS portal shall be accepted only if they strictly follow the relevant format of Springer CCIS series. The corresponding author will be intimated about status of their paper after being double blind review by at least Three experts in the field based on originality, significance, quality and clarity. The quality of papers will decide the publication of paper in post conference proceedings either in Springer CCIS series (Soft copy only) or in conference proceedings bearing ISBN number (Hard copy only). This means that only some selected papers will be published by Springer in their prestigious Communications in Computer and Information Science series (CCIS) series. Contributions will only be published in any of the two ICICCT-2018 post conference proceedings if at least one of the authors of the paper is registered before 15/4/2018.
